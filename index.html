About Us

The Paw Brew is an established coffee shop run by Maria and her furry friend Koda. They proud themselves in serving great quality coffee and food offering a wide range of products catering for anyone including gluten free products, vegan and healthy options.

More Than Coffee

Here at The Paw Brew we are constantly striving in delivering the best coffee! We always oversee all the processes until it is distributed to our shop and make sure it is of the best speciality grade. In our shop, we sell a wide range of coffee products including milky coffees and black coffees.

Charity

All of our puppies and dogs are rescues from Battersea Dogs, we pride ourselves in saying that rescue is our favourite breed. For more information on how you too can adopt a puppie please click here. You can also find out information on how to adopt.
