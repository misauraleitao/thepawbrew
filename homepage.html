About Us

The Paw Brew is an established coffee shop run by Maria and her furry friend Koda. They proud themselves in serving great quality coffee and food offering a wide range of products catering for anyone including gluten free products, vegan and healthy options.Here at the Paw Brew we have many puppies of which you can sit down and enjoy the company of them. Please note that all of our puppies and dogs are fully trained and very calm around people. Furthermore, you are also welcome to bring your own dog!

More than just good coffee

Here at The Paw Brew we are constantly striving in delevering the best coffee! We always oversee all the processes until it is distributed to our shop and make sure it is of the best speciality grade. In our shop, we sell a wide range of coffee products including milky coffees to black coffees including our filter coffee of the day and before you purchase you can ask a staff member to try before you buy. We only sell fair trade coffee and every season we travel abroad to the coffee farm where our season coffee is being grown.

Charity
